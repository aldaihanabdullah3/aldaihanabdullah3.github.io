Systems Security × Machine Learning

Abdullah is a PhD student in the Security & Machine Learning (SML) Lab at Imperial under the supervision of Dr. Sergio Maffeis. He received his MSc in Computer Science from Georgia Tech (2021) and his BSc from King Saud University (2014). His research focuses on applying large language models to automate systems security investigations and threat detection.
